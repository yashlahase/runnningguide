Tips for Beginners

Start running the right way with these helpful tips.

1. Start Slow

Begin with short runs and gradually increase your distance and speed to avoid injury.

2. Invest in Good Shoes

Choose running shoes that fit well and support your feet to enhance comfort and performance.

3. Warm-Up & Cool Down

Prepare your body with stretches and light jogging before running, and cool down afterward.

4. Stay Hydrated

Drink water before, during, and after your runs to maintain energy and avoid dehydration.
